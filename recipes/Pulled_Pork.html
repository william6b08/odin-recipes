Pulled Pork Burger recipe

Description

Pulled pork is an American barbecue dish, more specifically a dish of the Southern U.S., based on shredded barbecued pork shoulder. It is typically slow-smoked over wood (usually outdoors); indoor variations use a slow cooker.

The meat is then shredded manually and mixed with a sauce. It may be served on bread as a sandwich, or eaten on its own. In combination with spare ribs and brisket it is considered a part of the Texas Holy Trinity of Barbecue.>

Ingredients

  • 4 tbs burger sauce
  • 350g kaleslaw kit
  • 4 brioche gourmet burger buns
  • Simply heat pulled pork smokey BBQ

Steps

  1. Prepare pulled pork as per packet instructions. Pull meat apart and mix through sauce sachet.
  2. Pull apart brioche buns into halves and spread top and bottom of each bun with 1 tsb of burger sauce.
  3. Place pork and some of the kaleslaw into each bun. With remaining kaleslaw, mix through packet dressing and serve on the side.
